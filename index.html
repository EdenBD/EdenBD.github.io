  • Lyrics Audio Boost

    Combined Spotify audio feature and BERT word embeddings to consider both lyrics and audio for songs' sentiment predictions. I use hugginface pre-trained BERT as an embedding layer, and train an additional bidirectional GRU layer for the sentiment analysis regression task. You can check out the code and processed dataset.

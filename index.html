    I combine Spotify audio feature and BERT word embeddings to consider both lyrics and audio for the sentiment prediction. I use hugginface pre-trained BERT as an embedding layer, and train an additional bidirectional GRU layer for the sentiment analysis regression task. I open-sourced the code and processed dataset.
